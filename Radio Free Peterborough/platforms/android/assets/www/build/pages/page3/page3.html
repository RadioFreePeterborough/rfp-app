About the Radio
Free Peterborough Project

Radio Free Peterborough was founded in the summer of 2004 as the answer to the question "I wonder how much Peterborough music you could get together in one place?"

In partnership with Trent Radio, the Radio Free Peterborough / Local Content Archive has grown quite a lot from our humble beginnings! We run on 100% volunteer power with an operating budget of $0, using 100% Open Source Software. We did it - so can you!

Listen to Radio Free Peterborough with this app, online via our website at http://radiofreepeterborough.ca or all night every night on Trent Radio 92.7 FM in Peterborough and surrounding area
